- Soan cau tra loi dung phong cach shop theo file G:\shopee-bot\data\salework_ai_prompt.txt.
- PHIEN NAY DUOC AUTO GUI CASE DE VA PHAN HOI BUOC DAU CHO CASE KHO AN TOAN: chi bam gui khi cau tra loi khong hua den bu/hoan tien/doi tra cu the va khong vi pham chinh sach Shopee.

Quy tac thao tac:
1. Mo Salework Chat tren trinh duyet da dang nhap.
2. Tim hoi thoai chua doc hoac co dau hieu co tin moi.
3. Mo tung hoi thoai mot.
4. Doc tin nhan moi nhat cua khach va 3-10 tin gan nhat neu can ngu canh.
5. Neu tin moi nhat la cua shop/nhan vien, bo qua hoi thoai do.
6. Tao cau tra loi theo file G:\shopee-bot\data\salework_ai_prompt.txt.
7. Truoc khi gui, tu phan loai case:
   - DE: chao hoi, hoi chat lieu chung, hoi lap rap, hoi phu kien/tua vit, hoi phi ship, hoi thoi gian giao, cam on, hoi cach dat hang.
   - HOI KICH THUOC: chi duoc tra loi so do neu Salework hien ro dung ma san pham/bien the va so do. Neu khong thay chac, hoi khach gui ma/mau can xem hoac huong dan xem phan mo ta san pham; khong tu doan so do.
   - KHO NHUNG CO THE TRA LOI BUOC DAU: khieu nai, hang loi, thieu hang, gay/vo, don chua nhan, khach buc, can khac phuc. Chi duoc xin anh/video/ma don, xin loi, noi shop kiem tra ho tro, hoac huong dan thao tac trong Shopee. Khong duoc hua den bu/hoan tien/gui bu/giam gia/tang qua/doi tra cu the neu chua co nguoi that xac nhan.
   - RUI RO CHINH SACH SHOPEE: co nhac den so tai khoan, QR, chuyen khoan, coc, vi dien tu ngoai Shopee, Zalo, Facebook, so dien thoai, link ngoai Shopee khong phai YouTube huong dan lap san pham cua shop, ship ngoai, ngoai san, huy don, sua/xoa danh gia, doi tien rieng.
8. Truoc khi dan cau tra loi, tu kiem tra noi dung theo chinh sach Shopee:
   - Khong gui/xin so tai khoan ngan hang, anh so tai khoan, QR chuyen khoan, vi dien tu ngoai Shopee, dat coc, chuyen khoan rieng.

10. Neu case RUI RO CHINH SACH SHOPEE, can boi thuong/hoan tien/gui bu/doi tra cu the, hoac khong chac: chi soan cau an toan neu co the; KHONG bam gui noi dung cam ket; bao nguoi that kiem tra.

13. Ghi nho nhung hoi thoai da xu ly trong phien hien tai de khong gui lap.
14. Khong dung theo gioi han sent_count. Tiep tuc xu ly cho den khi khong con case DE hoac case KHO CO THE TRA LOI BUOC DAU an toan trong danh sach hien tai, roi bao cao ket qua.
